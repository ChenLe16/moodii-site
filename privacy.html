Privacy Policy for Moodii

Effective date: March 24, 2026

Moodii is designed to work primarily on your device.

Information stored by Moodii

Moodii stores your mood entries, feelings, notes, contexts, and app settings locally on your device.

If you choose a profile photo, Moodii stores a local reference to that selected photo on your device.

If you enable reminders, notification-related settings are stored locally so the app can schedule reminders on your device.

Information Moodii does not collect

Moodii does not require an account.

Moodii does not send your mood entries or notes to a remote server.

Moodii does not use third-party advertising or tracking.

Moodii does not sell your personal information.

Optional actions you control

You may export your data to a file and import it again later.

If you use the “Send feedback” feature, Moodii opens your email app so you can send a message to support. Any information you choose to send in that email is handled by your email provider.

Third-party services

Moodii relies on Apple and app platform system services to support features such as notifications, photo selection, file export/import, and email linking. Those services operate under their own terms and privacy practices.

Your choices

You can delete your entries and stored app data from inside the app.

You can change or remove your profile photo at any time.

You can disable reminders in the app or in iOS Settings.
